Purchased Goods and Services Emissions

Category 1: Upstream (cradle-to-gate) emissions from the production of goods and services purchased by the company.

S.no Date Quantity of Goods/Services Purchased (A) Unit Selection EF (Supplier-specific) (B) ETCO2eq Actions

Supplier-Specific Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Quantity Purchased × Supplier-Specific Emission Factor

This method uses product-level cradle-to-gate GHG inventory data directly from suppliers for the most accurate results.

Hybrid Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Supplier Scope 1 & 2 + (Material Inputs × Material EF) + (Transport Distance × Transport EF) + (Waste Outputs × Waste EF) + Other Emissions

This method combines supplier-specific data with secondary data to fill gaps.

Average-Data Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Mass/Quantity Purchased × Industry Average Emission Factor

This method uses secondary industry-average cradle-to-gate emission factors.
