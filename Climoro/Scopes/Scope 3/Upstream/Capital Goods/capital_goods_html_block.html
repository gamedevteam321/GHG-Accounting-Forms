Capital Goods Emissions

Category 2: Upstream (cradle-to-gate) emissions from producing capital goods (machinery, buildings, equipment, vehicles, IT assets, etc.) purchased by the company.

Supplier-Specific Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Quantity Purchased × Supplier-Specific Emission Factor

Use product-level cradle-to-gate GHG inventory data directly from the supplier of the capital good.

Hybrid Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Supplier Scope 1 & 2 + (Material Inputs × Material EF) + (Transport Distance × Transport EF) + (Waste Outputs × Waste EF) + Other Emissions

Combine supplier-specific data with secondary data to fill gaps for capital goods production.

Average-Data Method Formula:

Total Emissions = Mass/Quantity Purchased × Industry Average Emission Factor

Use secondary industry-average cradle-to-gate emission factors for the capital good’s material or product class.
